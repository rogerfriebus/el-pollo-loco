Controls

Desktop: Use the left and right arrow keys to move. Jump with the up arrow key or spacebar.

Throw bottle: Press D.

Fullscreen: Use the Fullscreen button in the game.

Mobile: In landscape mode, touch buttons appear for moving left, jumping, throwing and moving right.

Collect salsa bottles first. Only collected bottles can be thrown.

Rotate your device

El Pollo Loco is playable on mobile devices in landscape mode.
